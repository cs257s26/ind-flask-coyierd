Cowling Arboretum Spring Bird Count Dataset 2025

Filename: ArbSpringBirdCountData2025Update.xlsx
Received April 9, 2026 from Nancy Braker (nbraker@carleton.edu)


The dataset is divided into two sheets. 

For the Main sheet:
MOU = An abitrary number assigned by Mark McKone to help be able to sort the data
Year = the number of birds of that species observed during the count that year, or if a "p" an indication that the bird species was observed during the count but not at one of the stops, for example, while walking between stops.
% Years = The percent of years of the survey that this species was observed
No./yr. = Average number of birds of this species each year

For the sheets by year:
Common name (Scientific Name) = name of bird
MOU = An abitrary number assigned by Mark McKone to help be able to sort the data
1 through 17 = the observation stops, a number in this cell indicates how many of this species of bird was heard at this stop.
\# stops = the number of stops where the species of bird was observed
total = the number of that species of bird heard during the count
present = a "p" in this cell indicates that the bird species was observed during the count, but not at one of the stops
Notes = any additional notes made by the data recorder or data entry personnel that yet need to be resolved or added to the database.
